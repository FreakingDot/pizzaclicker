About Us!

Pizza Clicker is a project started by three 11th grade students of Codesters.Club. Our game's intention is to kill your bordeom! It is basic click and progress game, where one can cook different pizzas from cheese to truffle pizza. Play as long or as little as you like, your pizza wont spoil!
